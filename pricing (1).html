Catering & Events

Per-Event
Pricing

All services require a contract. You book per event — pay a flat base fee plus any applicable item or distance add-ons. Simple, transparent, and designed for one-time and occasional bookings.

Catering delivery is priced differently from recurring routes because the logistics are different. Each event has a unique pickup location, delivery window, guest count, and handling requirement.

How it works: Base delivery fee covers the run. Add-ons apply for extra stops, large guest counts, temperature-controlled items, or weekend/late-night timing. You always know the full cost before we confirm.

For recurring catering contracts (e.g. weekly hotel breakfast drops), we can build a custom retainer — contact Hamzah directly.
