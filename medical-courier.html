Common Questions

Medical Courier FAQ

We handle lab specimens, test results, prescriptions, pharmacy deliveries, medical records, referral documents, clinic supplies, and medical equipment. If you're unsure whether your item qualifies, call Hamzah directly and we'll confirm on the spot. We do not currently handle items requiring cryogenic or specialized temperature-controlled transport.

Our drivers are briefed on HIPAA sensitivity and handle all medical materials with the appropriate discretion — no unauthorized access, secure handoffs, and no casual handling of protected items or documents. We provide chain-of-custody documentation on every medical delivery. For facilities with specific compliance requirements, reach out to discuss your needs in detail before starting service.

Every medical delivery includes a pickup confirmation (time, location, item description), a transit log, and a delivery sign-off with timestamp and recipient. This documentation is provided to you after each completed run and kept on file. It gives your facility a complete record of every delivery handled by EL1Express.

For stat or urgent runs, contact Hamzah directly at (346) 668-6922. Same-day dispatch is available across Houston. A stat rush fee of $15 applies on top of on-demand pricing for priority handling. For facilities with frequent urgent needs, a contracted stat protocol can be built into your weekly plan.

Yes — multi-facility routes are a core part of what we do. Whether you need runs between a clinic and a reference lab, pharmacy-to-clinic transfers, or hospital network logistics across multiple Houston locations, we build the route to match your facility structure. Larger hospital systems and networks are quoted as custom enterprise contracts.

Scheduled contracts are invoiced weekly. Enterprise and hospital network accounts can arrange monthly billing, agreed upfront in the contract. On-demand runs are invoiced individually after each delivery. All billing goes directly through EL1Express — no platform fees, no third-party charges on top of your rate.
